Cleaning Tips

Pro Tip 1

Use microfiber cloths for dusting—they trap dirt without spreading it.

Pro Tip 2

Clean top-to-bottom to avoid re-cleaning surfaces.

Pro Tip 3

Use natural cleaners like vinegar and baking soda for safe, effective results.

Pro Tip 4

Vacuum before mopping to remove loose debris first.
